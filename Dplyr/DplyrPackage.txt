Dplyr Package

	A package that helps transform tabular data:
	library(dplyr)

	Dplyr Function:
	
	1) Select 
		select(datafram,column1,column2)
		select(datafram,-column2)
		select(datafram,column1:column3)
		select(datafram,constrains("String_value"))
		select(datafram,Start_with("String_value"))
		select(datafram,end_with("String_value"))
		select(datafram,matches("String_value"))
		select(datafram,everything())

	2) Filter	
		filter(datafram,condition)
		filter(datafram,condition,,condition2)

	You can use all comparison operator 	
	   eg, >,<,>=,<=,==,!=,%in%
